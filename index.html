🛕 Famous Temples in Tirupati

Discover the sacred downhill temples of Tirupati, each holding deep spiritual significance and ancient heritage.

Sri Padmavathi Ammavari Temple

Dedicated to Goddess Padmavathi (consort of Lord Venkateswara). Must-visit along with Tirumala temple.

Sri Govindaraja Swamy Temple

One of the largest temples in Tirupati. Dedicated to Lord Vishnu.

Kapila Theertham

Temple + waterfall dedicated to Lord Shiva. Only Shiva temple in Tirupati.

Sri Kodandarama Swamy Temple

Dedicated to Lord Rama, Sita, and Lakshmana.

ISKCON Tirupati

Dedicated to Lord Krishna. Peaceful and well-maintained.

Sri Kalyana Venkateswara Swamy Temple

Known for marriage-related prayers.

Appalayagunta Prasanna Venkateswara Temple

Less crowded, very peaceful temple.

Narayanavanam Temple

Believed place of Lord Venkateswara & Padmavathi marriage.

🚩 Popular Temples Near Tirupati

Explore the famous spiritual centers located within a short drive from Tirupati city.

Srikalahasti

Famous for Rahu-Ketu Puja and exquisite architecture dedicated to Lord Shiva.

Gudimalam

Home to one of the oldest known Shiva Lingams in the world, dating back to 2nd century BC.

Kanipakam

Sacred temple of Lord Ganesha, known for its self-manifested idol that is said to be growing.

Vellore

The stunning Golden Temple (Sripuram) coated in pure gold, set in a star-shaped lush green park.

Arunachalam

Lord Shiva's holy hill temple in Thiruvannamalai, famous for the Karthigai Deepam festival.

Kanchipuram

The city of thousand temples and world-famous silk, home to the Kamakshi Amman temple.

Tiruttani

One of the six sacred abodes of Lord Murugan, situated on a beautiful hill with 365 steps.

Narayanavanam

Ancient temple where the marriage of Lord Venkateswara and Goddess Padmavathi took place.
